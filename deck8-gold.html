8. Gholdengo Palkia (The Metal Surf)

Estimated Price: ~$35.40

Strategy Summary

This deck blends high draw consistency with infinite scaling damage. Gholdengo ex allows you to draw extra cards every turn using Coin Bonus, and its attack deals 50 damage for every basic energy you discard from your hand. By pairing it with Origin Forme Palkia VSTAR, you get access to Star Portal, which instantly recovers discarded energies right back to the board.

Turn-by-Turn Playbook

Turn 1 (The Global Search): Play Buddy-Buddy Poffin to grab Gimmighoul and Bidoof. Use Nest Ball to find Palkia V. Attach a Water or Metal Energy onto Palkia V to prepare for an early evolution.

Turn 2 (The Evolution Wave): Play Irida to search your deck for any Water Pokémon (like Palkia VSTAR) and an Item card (like a Superior Energy Retrieval or Ultra Ball) at the exact same time. Evolve your board into Gholdengo ex and Bibarel. Use Gholdengo's Coin Bonus and Bibarel's Industrious Incisors to fill your hand with raw card advantage.

Turn 3+ (Make It Rain): Fire off Gholdengo's Make It Rain attack. Use Superior Energy Retrieval to grab 4 energies back into your hand, then discard them all to instantly swing for 200+ damage. If your hand size drops too low, use Palkia VSTAR's Star Portal ability to pull Water Energies out of the discard pile and flood them onto your benched attackers.
