Strategy Info

The core strategy focuses on overwhelming the opponent with cheap single-prize Ancient Pokemon that hit massive numbers based on your discard pile. Use Squawkabilly ex and Explorer's Guidance early on to thin your deck and aggressively dump Ancient cards into the discard pile. Professor Sada's Vitality powers up two of your benched Ancient Pokemon at once while maintaining your card advantage. Once your discard pile is full of Ancient cards, Koraidon and Roaring Moon can swing for massive amounts of damage for very low energy costs, trading prize cards efficiently against heavier multi-prize ex targets.

Summary

An aggressive, highly resilient single-prize swarm archetype that uses rapid discard acceleration to scale its damage output into the late game.
