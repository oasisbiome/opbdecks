3. Klawf & Hisuian Electrode V "Zero-Energy" Burn

Estimated Price: ~$12.35

Strategy Info

Most status decks play expensive multi-prize ex engines. This rogue variant relies on Hisuian Electrode V, whose attack costs 0 Energy and deals 100 damage for every Special Condition affecting it. Spicy Seasoned Curry burns Electrode V while healing it, and Brute Bonnet poisons it, enabling 200+ damage attacks without attaching energy.

Summary

A status-swap deck that delivers high-damage attacks for zero energy attachments using self-inflicted Burn and Poison conditions.
