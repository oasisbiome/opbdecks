5. Crustle & Toedscruel "Discard Lock" Stall

Estimated Price: ~$6.70

Strategy Info

This anti-meta control list uses Toedscruel to prevent opponents from targeting cards in their discard pile (blocking Super Rods, Night Stretchers, and Dark Patch). Crustle acts as a high-HP wall. Backed by Crushing Hammer energy removal and Rigid Band damage reduction, it starves your opponent of key resources.

Summary

A resource-denial deck designed to lock down discard retrieval and energy attachments, forcing opponents to exhaust their deck resources.
