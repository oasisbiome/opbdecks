9. United Wings (The Budget Flock)

Estimated Price: ~$14.50

Strategy Summary

This is an incredibly cheap single-prize deck that scales damage based on the size of your discard pile. Pokémon with the United Wings attack deal more damage for every single card with the United Wings name sitting in your discard pile. You win by using high-speed drawing items to intentionally dump your own birds into the discard pile, creating a massive, low-cost swarm.

Turn-by-Turn Playbook

Turn 1: Set up card drawers like Radiant Greninja and get your core United Wings pieces onto the board or into the discard zone quickly to begin establishing scale mechanics.
