7. Roaring Moon ex (The Instant Knockout)

Estimated Price: ~$34.20

Strategy Summary

This is a hyper-aggressive deck built around raw, unchecked power. Roaring Moon ex features an attack called Frenzied Gouging, which instantly knocks out any opposing Pokémon on the field regardless of how much HP it has. You use Professor Sada's Vitality and Dark Patch to flood the board with Darkness Energy to score massive knockouts on turn one or two.

Turn-by-Turn Playbook

Turn 1 (The Dark Surge): Use Nest Balls to bench Roaring Moon ex, Radiant Greninja, and Squawkabilly ex. Use Greninja's Concealed Cards to discard a Darkness Energy and draw 2 cards. Activate Squawkabilly ex's ability to dump your hand and draw 6 new cards. Use Dark Patch to attach discarded energies directly to your benched Roaring Moon ex.

Turn 2 (The Calamity): Play Professor Sada's Vitality to draw 3 cards and attach energies from the discard pile to two of your Ancient Pokémon. Move Roaring Moon ex into the Active Spot and use Calamity Storm for up to 220 damage, discarding a Stadium card in play to clear away threats.

Turn 3+ (The Frenzied Gouging): If your opponent brings out a massive, unkillable Pokémon (like a 330 HP Charizard ex), use Frenzied Gouging to instantly destroy it. Note: This places 200 damage counters on your own Roaring Moon, so use Ancient Booster Capsules to boost its HP beforehand so it survives the backlash.
