6. Iron Thorns ex (The Ability Lock)

Estimated Price: ~$28.50

Strategy Summary

This deck focuses on total control by turning off your opponent's strategy before they can even start. Iron Thorns ex has an ability called Initialization, which completely shuts off the abilities of all Pokémon with a Rule Box (like Charizard ex, Regidrago, and Ogerpon) as long as it is in the Active Spot. You use Electric Generator to power it up fast and win by slowing the game to a crawl.

Turn-by-Turn Playbook

Turn 1 (The Iron Wall): Start with an Iron Thorns ex in your Active Spot to immediately lock down your opponent's abilities. Use Techno Radar or Nest Ball to put two backup Iron Thorns ex onto your bench. Fire off Electric Generator to try and pull Lightning Energies straight from your deck to your Pokémon.

Turn 2 (Charging Up): Attach a Future Booster Energy Capsule to your active Thorns to give it free retreat and a damage boost. Use more Electric Generators or manually attach your turn's energy. Play Arven or Iono to disrupt your opponent's hand while keeping your resources moving.

Turn 3+ (The Volt Cyclone): Attack with Volt Cyclone for 140 damage, then move an energy card from your Active Pokémon to one of your benched Iron Thorns ex to keep them all safely charged. Use Crushing Hammer and Lost Vacuum to discard your opponent's tools and energies, keeping them completely trapped under your ability lock.
