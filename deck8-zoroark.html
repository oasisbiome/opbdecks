Strategy Info

This strategy revolves around maximizing the mechanics of N-themed Pokémon. Your primary engine uses N's Plan to rapidly cycle cards into your discard pile while attaching basic Darkness or Fire energy elements directly to your benched setup. N's Zoroark ex acts as an aggressive primary attacker, while N's Reshiram offers incredible high-damage coverage options. Use Jacq to rapidly pull your Stage 1 evolutions, and secure late-game recovery lines with Night Stretcher to maintain your board presence.

Summary

A thematic, hard-hitting archetype that utilizes N's Plan to accelerate energy onto heavy N-series attackers, catching opponents off-guard with Zoroark's adaptive illusions.
