2. Poison-Curse Gengar ex & Hisuian Sneasler

Estimated Price: ~$9.55

Strategy Info

While mainstream Gengar ex builds focus on energy disruption, this build turns Gengar ex into a passive damage engine. Gengar ex’s Gnawing Curse deals 20 damage every time your opponent attaches an energy card. Pairing it with Brute Bonnet and Radiant Hisuian Sneasler creates an un-healable passive poison tick that deals an extra 30–50 damage between turns.

Summary

A chip-damage control deck that punishes your opponent with poison damage every turn while Gengar ex penalizes their energy attachments.
