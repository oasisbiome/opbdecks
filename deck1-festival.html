1. Seaking & Dipplin "Double-Draw" Festival

Estimated Price: ~$7.90

Strategy Info

Standard Festival Lead lists stick exclusively to Grass attacks. This hybrid build techs in Seaking (PRE) alongside Dipplin. Under Festival Grounds, any Pokémon with Festival Lead gets two attacks per turn. Seaking’s attack deals 60 damage and draws 2 cards; executing it twice yields 120 damage and +4 cards in hand for just a single Water Energy. Thwackey acts as a tutored search engine to retrieve exact tools (Defiance Band) or counter-supporters every turn.

Summary

An aggressive single-prize deck that combines Dipplin’s multi-prize KO potential with Seaking’s attack-based draw engine to overwhelm meta decks before they set up.
