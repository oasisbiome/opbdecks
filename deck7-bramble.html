Crimson Bramble

Estimated Price: $34.70 total.

Tier: Tier 2.

Strategy Info

This deck aims to completely shut down popular item-heavy and energy-attach meta strategies by leveraging Toedscruel ex's protective abilities. While Toedscruel ex blocks your opponent from manipulating cards out of their discard pile, your primary attacker is Brambleghast. Brambleghast's damage scale increases drastically for every prize card your opponent has taken. By pairing Brambleghast with Blunder Policy, any unlucky coin flips on your attacks immediately translate into massive card draw, keeping your hand fully fueled with recovery options and extra energy to continuously flood the field with threats.

Summary

A defensive, anti-meta control setup that forces opponents into slow prize trades before punishing them with scaling late-game counterattacks.
